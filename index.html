⚕️ Medical Disclaimer

MamaShield is not a medical service. It is a personal scheduling tool to help you track your baby's Nigerian NPI immunization appointments.

MamaShield does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. All vaccine schedule information is sourced from Nigeria's National Programme on Immunization (NPI) 2025–2026 schedule, published by the NPHCDA.

MamaShield is not affiliated with the NMA, MDCN, or NPHCDA. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for all medical decisions. If your baby is unwell, seek immediate medical attention.

🔒 Your Data Privacy — Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023

In compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023:

What we collect: Baby's name, date of birth, sex, blood group (optional), mother's name (optional), and clinic name (optional).

Where your data is stored: Only on your device. MamaShield uses your phone's secure local app storage (IndexedDB). Your data is never transmitted to any server. It persists on your device between sessions and survives browser cache clearing.

To erase your data: Use the "Clear my data" button inside the tracker at any time.

Your rights under NDPA 2023: You may withdraw consent and erase your data at any time.
